# v0.2.0 — release notes

Deletes the trail of crumbs left on your computer as you browse around
the web. Allows you to select things you want to delete. Works on
demand or automatically on close, or, for the most paranoid among us,
when your browser is left idle for some time.

Still has some bugs. Doesn't have any ads, begging for money, CRAP,
and things to invade your privacy.

No Viruses as far as I know. Works OK now. Still trying to get it to
work better.
